A quality stainless steel bolt assortment is the backbone of any serious workshop or job site kit. We evaluated multiple kits across corrosion resistance, thread accuracy, and value per piece count.

What We Tested

We looked at kits ranging from 200 to 1,000+ pieces, covering common sizes from M3 to M10 and SAE equivalents. Key criteria: 304 vs 316 stainless grade, case quality, and whether the size labeling was accurate.

Verdict

Hex Nut Assortment Kit — Full Review

⭐ 4.6  ·  $22.99

Hex nuts are the most-reached-for fastener in any kit. A good assortment covers the sizes you actually need without drowning you in sizes you'll never use.

What We Tested

We tested kits from budget import brands up to USA-made options. Focus areas: thread pitch accuracy, hardness grade markings, and whether the storage case held up after repeated use.

Verdict

Grade 5 or Grade 8 marked nuts are worth the small premium. Unmarked import nuts are fine for light-duty work but skip them for anything structural. A well-organized case saves more time than the price difference costs.
